Learn the Basics

Short, plain-English explanations to get you started.

Recognising Anxiety

Racing thoughts, restlessness, tight chest, or trouble sleeping can be signs of anxiety. Small, steady steps help.

Try 4‑7‑8 breathing, limit caffeine later in the day, and write down worries to park them.

Managing Stress

Stress is the body’s response to pressure. Short bursts can be useful; long periods can be draining.

Use short breaks, time-boxing (25 mins focus, 5 mins rest), hydration, and a quick walk.

Seeking Help

Talking to a GP, a helpline, or someone you trust is a strong first step — you don’t have to do it alone.

Keep notes of how you’ve been feeling, bring 1–2 examples, and ask about local support options.

Stress Mini‑Guide: Simple Steps Today

Try one or two ideas — small actions add up.

  • 💨 4‑7‑8 breathing: Inhale 4, hold 7, exhale 8 — repeat 4 rounds.
  • 🧭 Time‑box: 25 minutes focused work, 5 minutes rest — repeat 3–4 times.
  • 🚶 Move: 10‑minute walk or gentle stretch to reset the nervous system.
  • 💧 Hydrate: A glass of water now, then keep a bottle nearby.
  • 🛏️ Sleep basics: Wind down, dim lights, avoid screens 30 minutes before bed.
  • 📝 Journaling: Write 3 lines: “What I feel / Why / One kind step I can take.”
